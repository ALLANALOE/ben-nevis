Our School

Welcome to Ben-Nevis Private School

Ben-Nevis Private School was established in the year 2003 and later registered in 2005. We began our journey with humble beginnings, starting with an enrolment of seven students.

Our institution gained recognition and widespread acclaim in 2005 due to a remarkable 100% pass rate in the Malawi School Certificate of Education Examination (MSCE).

We are proudly located in Chiradzulu District, where we have permanent and modern facilities. As a Small and Medium Enterprise (SME), we are dedicated to providing quality education while working closely with the community. Through collaboration with village heads, we extend educational opportunities to deserving students in the area.

Ben-Nevis Private School is under the sole proprietorship of Mr. Wellington Ben Navitcha, whose vision and commitment have been instrumental in our success.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide "National Education to Visionary Industrious Students (NEVIS)". We are committed to nurturing and empowering our students to achieve their full potential and become future leaders.
